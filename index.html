Oat Meal

Ingredients:

  • oats
  • Milk
  • Almonds
  • Walnuts
  • chia seed

Instructions

  1. Take a vessel
  2. Pour half litre of milk into vessel
  3. After boiling add 250 grams of oats into that milk
  4. keep it in low flame for 15-20 minitues
  5. Take it into a serving bowl and add wallnuts and almonds
